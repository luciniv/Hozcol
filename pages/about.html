About us
HOZCOL began as a passion project between two community managers. With over five years of industry experience, we decided to use our expertise for good.  We continued to serve our communities, giving server members safe and engaging places to enjoy. As time passed, we further refined our vision and approach to helping others. Today, HOZCOL fills that need. Whether you want to revive a past server, enhance your current community, or are just starting out, we can help. We’re here to support owners and give back to the communities that inspired us to begin with.
Our Mission By prioritizing effective, empathetic and proactive community management, HOZCOL aims to empower creators and businesses to focus on their passions while we handle the complexities of community building and maintenance. You shouldn't have to choose between your work and your community. No more neglect for your community, no more sacrifice for you. That equilibrium is our promise.
Why are our services free? …because everyone deserves a community to be proud of.
We’re Discord users at heart, creating and helping communities on a platform we love. When we can support a server or build one from scratch, we’re adding to a growing list of safe, welcoming spaces online. 
Offering our services for free makes proper community care vastly more accessible. Simply put, we think cost should never constrain a community’s wellness.

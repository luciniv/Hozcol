FAQ
When can I expect a response?
If you've provided us with an email, we'll get back to you in 1-2 business days. We're also happy to chat via Discord.
What can I contact your team for?
Anything related to communities! We're happy to answer questions and give advice. If it's not related to communities but you think we can help, let us know!
What's the Community Owner Intake Form for?
We use it to understand your vision for your community and collect the information we need to do things right. You can expedite the process of our involvement in your community by completing it before you contact us. If you'd prefer to wait, we can send you the form to complete later.
